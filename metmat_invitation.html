Showcase Your Talent! (Technical Events) 🧠✨

Paper Presentation:

  • Present your innovative ideas in 3 to 5 minutes!
  • Followed by a 2 to 3-minute Question-and-Answer session – impress us!
  • **Individual participants** or **Teams of up to two members** allowed!
  • **Abstract Submission Deadline:** April 14, 2025 (Monday) – Don't miss it!

Poster Presentation:

  • Create an awesome A3 format poster (297mm x 420mm)!
  • Ensure clear, readable fonts and incorporate high-quality visuals (images, charts, diagrams)!
